§ 02

How I work

Three principles that shape every project I take on, from discovery to launch.

01

Research before pixels.

Every interface I ship is grounded in evidence. I run quantitative surveys to size the problem and qualitative interviews to understand the why. The Stori referral redesign started with a single insight from six interviews — users felt ashamed to refer — that reframed the entire program.

02

Reframe, don't just refine.

Senior design isn't faster pixel pushing — it's questioning whether the brief is right. The best contribution I made on Stori wasn't a screen. It was the strategic shift from a competitive leaderboard to a mission-based system, which unlocked +22.85% growth.

03

Design for the metric that matters.

I've worked in growth squads, where every screen has a target. I'm fluent in conversion funnels, retention cohorts, and post-launch surveys. I write success criteria before I open Figma, and I measure what I shipped.
